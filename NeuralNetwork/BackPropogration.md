# Đạo hàm ma trận :
1. **Scalar-By-Vector**
$$\frac{\partial y}{\partial \mathbf{x}} = \left[\frac{\partial y}{\partial x_1} \\ \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_2} \cdots \frac{\partial y}{\partial x_n}\right]$$
2. **Vector-By-Vector**
$$\frac{\partial \mathbf{y}}{\partial \mathbf{x}} = \begin{bmatrix}\frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_2} & \cdots &\frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_n}\\ \\ \frac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \frac{\partial y_2}{\partial x_n}\\ \\ \vdots & \vdots & \\ \\ \frac{\partial y_1}{\partial x_1}\end{bmatrix}$$
# Motivation :
> Đơn giản là cực tiểu hóa hàm lỗi.
